Objectives
People development

Aiming to develop the skills and competencies of tourism managers and employees by providing them with a new training method on how to deal with customers with special needs on a global scale.

Course Enhancement

To improve the contain of the training courses by adding a new chapter regarding how to deal with clients with special needs

Promote inclusion

To promote social inclusion of people with special needs by offering them qualified services

Overcome discrimination

To combat discrimination by training other people how to deal with people with special needs

Unity

To develop partnerships on the local, national and international level between VET providers and actors on the labour market.
