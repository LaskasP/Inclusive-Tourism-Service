Outputs
O1. Improved training course in Domain of Tourism

  • Create a new chapter regarding how to deal with clients with special needs

  • Consult organizations active in socio-economic sector and the domain of tourism

  • Experienced and skilled people in the domain of tourism will produce a guidance to feed the training course

  • The output will be evaluated within organizations and businesses that operate in the tourism sector

O2. Instruction Guide for Hotel Employees

  • Trained and experienced managers will work together with the project's team on the creation of the guide after attending the short-term training

  • With the help of this created output the managers will be able to train their own staff and offering qualified services to those with special needs

  • Implementation of the guidance will take place in real workspaces for testing and evaluation

  • The final output will be produced based on corrections after the feedback process

O3. Online Diary of those who participated at the training

  • Our goal is to make the learning activity as much accessible as possible

  • After attending the short-term training the participants will share their experiences online promoting social inclusion of people with special needs
